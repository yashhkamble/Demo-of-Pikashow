Pikashow

Pikashow is a free Android application that you can use to stream movies, live TV, sports, web-series, TV shows, and much more.

Basically, Pikashow is a free Android application that you can use to stream movies, live TV, sports, web-series, TV shows, and much more. Although everyone can use this app, it’s mainly for Indian viewers who have a keen interest in movies. You can stream your favorite shows and series from more than platforms like Hotstar, Netflix, Amazon Prime, and so on. The best part of this app is it is very well categorized which makes it very easy for the users. Although you may find various apps like Hotstar, Pura TV, and so on but this application stands away from its competitors due to its features.

What makes Pikashow Special?

Pikashow is different from others due to its features so have a look at its features:

  • This app supports almost all types of devices which range from Smart TV to Firesticks. So just download the app and enjoy your favorite shows.
  • You don’t need WiFi or something like that you can easily stream your favorite show with your mobile data.
  • They are different categories to choose from whether you are a movie lover or a sports geek Pikashow app got you covered.
  • Easy to use: All the latest movies are displayed on the homepage.
